# Origin

This repo was created as part of the GTM Systems, Traffic Integrity, and Digital Intelligence expansion wave for the Kinetic Gain portfolio.

## Intent

The goal was to show that access-log analysis belongs in the same conversation as traffic filtering and growth-signal integrity. Rather than treating logs like a back-office operations detail, the repo turns them into a business-facing control plane:

- suspicious request patterns are scored before they distort incident interpretation
- source fingerprints are explained in commercial language
- Growth and Security can see the cost of leaving anomalous traffic unresolved

## Positioning

`anomaly-log-hunter` supports the `Traffic Integrity` pillar:

- role level: `Architect`
- signal metric: `92% Threat Mitigation`
- focus: bot protection, fraud prevention, clean traffic pipelines

It also reinforces the broader public story:

- Boston Enterprise Technologist
- Pipeline Scaling & Threat Mitigation
